Why Your Donation Matters

The Capps-Batavia Fire Department is a volunteer department that depends on community support to purchase and maintain the equipment needed to protect our district.

Your generosity directly funds:

  • Firefighting equipment and protective gear
  • Vehicle maintenance and apparatus upkeep
  • Firefighter training and certification
  • Station operations and utilities
