Our Mission

The Mission of the Capps Batavia Fire Department is to provide fire prevention and extinguishing services, emergency medical services, and as well as to support emergency services, first to the residents of the District, second the districts with which we hold agreements, and third to any who request our assistance.

From the department bylaws. In place since 1977, updated only to add emergency medical services.

History

The Capps-Batavia Fire Department has a long history of serving the rural communities of Boone County. Founded by local volunteers committed to protecting their neighbors, the department has grown over the years to meet the needs of the community.

Today, we continue that tradition of community service with a dedicated team of volunteer firefighters trained to handle a wide range of emergencies.
