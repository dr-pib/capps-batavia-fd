Why Volunteer?

When a fire starts or a 911 call goes out in our district, it's neighbors who answer. Not a crew from somewhere else. Us. The people who live here, raise families here, and choose to be ready when someone's worst day arrives.

Volunteering with the Capps-Batavia Fire Department is a real way to serve the place you call home, and to do work that genuinely matters. You'll train alongside a close crew, learn skills most people never get the chance to, and be there for your neighbors when it counts. It's hands-on, it's challenging, and it's some of the most rewarding work you'll ever do.

No experience is necessary. We provide the training and the certifications that turn a willing volunteer into a capable firefighter and first responder. What you bring is the willingness to show up, learn, and look out for the people around you.

What you'll gain

  • Free, hands-on training and certifications you keep for life
  • Real skills and confidence that carry into everything else you do
  • A crew that has your back, and a place where you belong
  • A direct, visible way to protect the community you love

There's a place here for anyone ready to step up. Whether you can give a few hours a month or a lot more, whether you're brand new or bringing experience from somewhere else, you'll be trained, supported, and welcomed. Our experienced members will help you learn the ropes, and before long, you'll be the one others look to.

What We Do

Firefighting

Structural, wildland, and vehicle fire suppression.

Emergency Response

First response to accidents, medical emergencies, and hazardous situations.

Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years of age
  • Reside in or near the Capps-Batavia Fire District
  • Pass a background check
  • Willing to complete required training during the first year you're on the department
  • Able to respond to calls during your availability

How to Apply

Applications must be filled out at a training meeting. Monthly training meetings are held on the third Monday of each month at 7:00pm at the Capps Fire Station, 4391 Old Capps Rd, Harrison, AR 72601. Rarely, monthly training meetings are rescheduled.

If you're interested in attending to meet our personnel and complete an application, fill out the form below and choose the training meeting you plan to attend. We'll be expecting you, and we have your contact information in the rare case a meeting is rescheduled.
