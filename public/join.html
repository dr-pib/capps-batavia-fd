Why Volunteer?

Volunteering with the Capps-Batavia Fire Department is one of the most meaningful things you can do for your community. Our volunteers are the backbone of fire protection and emergency response in our district.

No experience is necessary — we provide training. What we need is your dedication and commitment to serving your neighbors.

What We Do

🔥

Firefighting

Structural, wildland, and vehicle fire suppression.

🚑

Emergency Response

First response to accidents, medical emergencies, and hazardous situations.

🏘️

Community Service

Public education, fire prevention, and community events.
