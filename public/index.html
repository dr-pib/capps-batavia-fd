Our Mission

Neighbors protecting neighbors across 67 square miles of the Ozarks.

The Capps-Batavia Fire Department is staffed entirely by volunteers who give their time and training to protect the lives and property of everyone in our district. We respond to fires, accidents, and emergencies — and we're always looking for dedicated people to stand alongside us.
